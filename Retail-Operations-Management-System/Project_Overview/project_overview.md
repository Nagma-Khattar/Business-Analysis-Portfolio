# 2. Project Background

NorthStar Retail Inc. is a growing retail organization that requires a centralized system to improve its daily business operations. Currently, different departments manage inventory, sales, employees, customers, and reporting activities using disconnected processes and multiple systems.

The organization requires an integrated Retail Operations Management System (ROMS) to streamline operations, improve data accuracy, enhance reporting capabilities, and support better business decision-making.

---

# 3. Business Problem

The current operational processes have several challenges:

- Limited visibility into inventory levels and product availability.
- Manual tracking of sales and operational information.
- Difficulty generating accurate and timely reports.
- Data duplication across different departments.
- Lack of centralized information management.
- Increased effort required for operational decision-making.

These challenges impact productivity, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

---

# 4. Project Objectives

The objectives of the Retail Operations Management System are:

- Develop a centralized platform for managing retail operations.
- Improve inventory tracking and management.
- Enable efficient sales and customer management.
- Provide accurate reporting and analytics capabilities.
- Reduce manual processes and data duplication.
- Improve communication between business departments.
- Support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 5. Project Scope

## In Scope

The system will include:

### Inventory Management
- Product creation and maintenance.
- Stock level tracking.
- Inventory updates.
- Low-stock notifications.

### Sales Management
- Sales transaction recording.
- Order management.
- Sales reporting.

### Customer Management
- Customer information management.
- Customer purchase history.

### Employee Management
- Employee records.
- Department information.
- Role management.

### Reporting and Analytics
- Operational reports.
- Sales reports.
- Inventory reports.

---

# 6. Out of Scope

The following items are not included in the initial release:

- Online payment gateway integration.
- Mobile application development.
- Supplier management.
- Advanced artificial intelligence forecasting.
- External third-party system integrations.

---

# 7. Expected Benefits

The implementation of ROMS will provide:

- Improved operational efficiency.
- Better inventory control.
- Faster access to business information.
- Improved reporting accuracy.
- Reduced manual effort.
- Enhanced customer service.
- Better decision-making through analytics.

---

# 8. Project Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful when:

- Business users can manage retail operations through a centralized system.
- Inventory information is accurate and easily accessible.
- Reports can be generated efficiently.
- System users successfully complete required workflows.
- The solution meets defined business requirements.

---

# 9. Assumptions

The project assumes:

- Users will receive appropriate system training.
- Required business information will be available.
