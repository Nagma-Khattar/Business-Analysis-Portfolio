# Business Case

## Retail Operations Management System (ROMS)

**Client:** NorthStar Retail Inc.  
**Document Type:** Business Case  
**Prepared By:** Nagma Khattar  

---

# 1. Executive Summary

NorthStar Retail Inc. is experiencing operational challenges due to fragmented processes used for managing inventory, sales, customer information, and business reporting.

The Retail Operations Management System (ROMS) is proposed as an integrated solution to centralize retail operations, improve operational efficiency, enhance data accuracy, and provide better visibility into business performance.

This business case evaluates the need, benefits, risks, and expected value of implementing ROMS.

---

# 2. Current Business Situation

NorthStar Retail Inc. currently manages several retail activities through a combination of manual processes, spreadsheets, and disconnected systems.

Different departments maintain their own information, resulting in limited data sharing and inconsistent reporting.

Key operational areas affected include:

- Inventory management
- Sales tracking
- Customer information management
- Employee management
- Business reporting

---

# 3. Problem Statement

The organization faces the following business challenges:

## Inventory Challenges

- Limited real-time visibility of inventory levels.
- Difficulty identifying low-stock products.
- Manual inventory updates increase errors.

## Reporting Challenges

- Reports require significant manual effort.
- Business leaders do not always have timely information.
- Data analysis is time-consuming.

## Operational Challenges

- Duplicate data entry across departments.
- Lack of centralized information.
- Inefficient communication between teams.

---

# 4. Business Need

NorthStar Retail Inc. requires a centralized Retail Operations Management System that will:

- Improve operational visibility.
- Reduce manual processes.
- Provide accurate and timely information.
- Support better business decisions.
- Improve customer service.
- Enable future technology enhancements.

---

# 5. Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is the development of ROMS, an integrated enterprise application that will support:

## Inventory Management

- Product management.
- Stock tracking.
- Inventory updates.
- Inventory reporting.

## Sales Management

- Sales transaction management.
- Order tracking.
- Sales performance reporting.

## Customer Management

- Customer profile management.
- Purchase history tracking.

## Employee Management

- Employee records.
- Department management.
- User roles.

## Analytics and Reporting

- Business dashboards.
- Operational reports.
- Performance insights.

---

# 6. Business Objectives

The project objectives are:

| Objective | Description |
|---|---|
| Improve Efficiency | Reduce manual operational activities |
| Improve Accuracy | Minimize data errors and duplication |
| Enhance Reporting | Provide timely business insights |
| Increase Visibility | Enable centralized information access |
| Support Growth | Provide scalable technology platform |

---

# 7. Expected Business Benefits

## Operational Benefits

- Faster business processes.
- Reduced administrative effort.
- Improved inventory control.

## Management Benefits

- Better decision-making.
- Improved reporting capabilities.
- Real-time business visibility.

## Customer Benefits

- Improved service quality.
- Faster response to customer needs.

---

# 8. Alternatives Considered

## Option 1: Continue Existing Processes

Advantages:
- No immediate investment required.

Disadvantages:
- Existing inefficiencies remain.
- Limited scalability.
- Continued manual effort.

---

## Option 2: Purchase an Existing Retail Solution

Advantages:
- Faster implementation.
- Existing features available.

Disadvantages:
- Customization limitations.
- Higher licensing costs.

---

## Option 3: Develop ROMS

Advantages:
- Customized solution.
- Better alignment with business needs.
- Future scalability.

Disadvantages:
- Requires development effort.
- Requires ongoing maintenance.

---

# 9. Risks and Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| User adoption challenges | Provide training and documentation |
| Data migration issues | Validate and clean data before migration |
| Project delays | Use Agile planning and regular reviews |
| Changing requirements | Maintain continuous stakeholder communication |

---

# 10. Recommendation

Based on the identified business challenges and expected benefits, implementing the Retail Operations Management System is recommended.

The solution will help NorthStar Retail Inc. improve operational efficiency, enhance reporting capabilities, and create a scalable foundation for future growth.
