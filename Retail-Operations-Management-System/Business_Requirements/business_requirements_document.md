# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Retail Operations Management System (ROMS)

**Client:** NorthStar Retail Inc.  
**Document Type:** Business Requirements Document  
**Prepared By:** Nagma Khattar  

---

# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this Business Requirements Document (BRD) is to define the high-level business needs and objectives for the Retail Operations Management System (ROMS).

This document establishes the business expectations that will guide system design, development, testing, and implementation.

---

# 2. Business Goals

NorthStar Retail Inc. aims to:

- Improve operational efficiency.
- Centralize retail information.
- Reduce manual business processes.
- Improve data accuracy.
- Enable better reporting and decision-making.
- Provide a scalable technology solution.

---

# 3. Business Requirements

## BR-001: Centralized Information Management

**Description:**

The organization requires a centralized system to manage retail operational information including products, inventory, customers, employees, and sales.

**Business Value:**

Improves data accessibility and reduces duplication.

**Priority:**

High

---

## BR-002: Inventory Visibility

**Description:**

The system must provide accurate visibility into current inventory levels.

**Business Value:**

Helps prevent stock shortages and improves inventory planning.

**Priority:**

High

---

## BR-003: Sales Management Capability

**Description:**

The system should support recording and tracking sales transactions.

**Business Value:**

Improves sales monitoring and operational reporting.

**Priority:**

High

---

## BR-004: Customer Information Management

**Description:**

The system should maintain customer information and purchase history.

**Business Value:**

Supports improved customer service and relationship management.

**Priority:**

Medium

---

## BR-005: Employee Management

**Description:**

The system should maintain employee information and organizational roles.

**Business Value:**

Improves employee record management.

**Priority:**

Medium

---

## BR-006: Reporting and Analytics

**Description:**

The system should provide business reports for operational and management decision-making.

**Business Value:**

Enables data-driven decisions.

**Priority:**

High

---

## BR-007: Security and Access Control

**Description:**

The system must protect business information through controlled access based on user roles.

**Business Value:**

Ensures confidentiality and data protection.

**Priority:**

High

---

# 4. Business Rules

| Rule ID | Business Rule |
|---|---|
| BRULE-001 | Each product must have a unique product identifier |
| BRULE-002 | Inventory quantities must be updated after sales transactions |
| BRULE-003 | Users must have authorized access based on their role |
| BRULE-004 | Sales transactions must contain customer and product information |
| BRULE-005 | Reports must use accurate and validated data |
