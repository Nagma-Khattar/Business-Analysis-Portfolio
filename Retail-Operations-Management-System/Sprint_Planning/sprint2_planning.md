# Sprint 2 – Customer and Reporting Enhancement

## Sprint Goal

Deliver customer management and reporting capabilities to maintain customer information, analyze sales performance, and support data-driven business decisions.

## Sprint Duration

2 Weeks

## Selected User Stories

- ROMS-13 Create Customer Profile
- ROMS-12 View Sales Report
- ROMS-14 Generate Business Reports

## Deferred Stories

No deferred stories.

## Prioritization Reason

Sprint 2 focuses on extending the core retail system with customer management and reporting capabilities.

Customer profile management enables the system to maintain customer information and associate customers with retail activities. Sales reporting uses transaction data generated from Sprint 1 to provide visibility into sales performance.

Business reports combine information from sales, inventory, and customer modules to support management decision-making.

The Sprint 2 stories were prioritized after completing the foundational functionality from Sprint 1, including authentication, inventory management, and sales transaction processing.
