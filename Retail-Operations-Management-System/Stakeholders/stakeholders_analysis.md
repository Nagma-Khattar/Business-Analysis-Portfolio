# Stakeholder Analysis

## Retail Operations Management System (ROMS)

**Client:** NorthStar Retail Inc.  
**Document Type:** Stakeholder Analysis  
**Prepared By:** Nagma Khattar  

---

# 1. Introduction

Stakeholder analysis identifies individuals, groups, and organizations that are impacted by the Retail Operations Management System (ROMS).

Understanding stakeholder needs is essential for successful requirements gathering, communication planning, solution design, and project delivery.

---

# 2. Stakeholder Identification Approach

Stakeholders were identified by analyzing:

- Business processes affected by the system.
- Users who interact with the solution.
- Individuals responsible for decision-making.
- Teams involved in development, implementation, and support.

---

# 3. Stakeholder Register

| Stakeholder | Role | Interest | Influence | Responsibilities |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Executive Sponsor | Project Sponsor | High | High | Provides funding, approves major decisions |
| Store Manager | Business User | High | Medium | Defines operational requirements |
| Inventory Manager | Business User | High | Medium | Provides inventory management needs |
| Sales Team | End User | Medium | Low | Uses sales management features |
| Customer Service Team | End User | Medium | Low | Manages customer information |
| Finance Department | Business User | Medium | Medium | Uses financial reports |
| Project Manager | Project Leadership | High | High | Manages timeline, resources, risks |
| Business Analyst | Requirements Lead | High | Medium | Elicits and documents requirements |
| System Analyst | Technical Lead | Medium | High | Designs system architecture |
| Developers | Technical Team | Medium | Medium | Develops system functionality |
| QA Engineers | Quality Team | Medium | Medium | Validates system quality |
| IT Support Team | Operations | Medium | Medium | Supports deployed application |

---

# 4. Stakeholder Roles and Expectations

## Executive Sponsor

### Expectations:
- Project delivers business value.
- Budget and timeline are controlled.
- Business objectives are achieved.

---

## Store Manager

### Expectations:
- Easy access to inventory information.
- Improved operational visibility.
- Faster reporting.

---

## Inventory Manager

### Expectations:
- Accurate stock information.
- Automated inventory updates.
- Better inventory tracking.

---

## Sales Team

### Expectations:
- Simple sales transaction process.
- Quick access to product information.
- Improved customer service.

---

## Business Analyst

### Responsibilities:

- Conduct stakeholder interviews.
- Gather requirements.
- Document business needs.
- Manage requirement changes.
- Communicate between business and technical teams.

---

## Development Team

### Expectations:

- Clear requirements.
- Complete documentation.
- Defined acceptance criteria.

---

# 5. Stakeholder Communication Plan

| Stakeholder Group | Communication Method | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Executive Sponsor | Status Reports | Bi-weekly |
| Business Users | Requirement Workshops | Weekly |
| Project Team | Sprint Meetings | Daily/Weekly |
| QA Team | Testing Reviews | During Testing Phase |
| IT Support | Deployment Meetings | Before Release |

---

# 6. Stakeholder Management Strategy

The project team will:

- Maintain regular communication with stakeholders.
- Validate requirements before development.
- Address concerns early.
- Collect feedback throughout the project lifecycle.
- Ensure stakeholder involvement during testing and implementation.

---

# 7. Conclusion

Effective stakeholder management is critical to ensure ROMS meets business expectations and delivers measurable value to NorthStar Retail Inc.
