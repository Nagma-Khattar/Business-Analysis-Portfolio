# Challenges / Areas for Improvement

## 1. Story Point Estimation

Story point estimates were not added during Sprint 2 planning.

This was identified as a continuing improvement area from Sprint 1.

### Improvement Action:
- Add story point estimates during backlog refinement before starting future sprints.
- Discuss complexity, effort, and dependencies with the team before sprint planning.

---

## 2. Acceptance Criteria Improvement

Acceptance criteria should be reviewed earlier with developers and QA to ensure:

- Requirements are clear.
- Testing scenarios are aligned.
- Expected outcomes are measurable.

---

## 3. Sprint Planning Improvement

Continue improving sprint planning by:

- Reviewing dependencies before committing stories.
- Ensuring sprint capacity matches workload.
- Prioritizing high-value business features.
